1. Calculating Crop ET: Crop Coefficient (Kc) Approach

Formula (concept): ETc = Kc × ET0 where ET0 is reference evapotranspiration (e.g., grass reference).

6. Radiation Method

These methods use net radiation (Rn) and sometimes soil heat flux (G):
Example: ET = (c × Rn)/(λ) (λ = latent heat of vaporization).

7. Penman Method

9. FAO Penman–Monteith Equation (FAO-56)

Standardized FAO-56 form for reference ET (ETo) — requires net radiation, air temperature, wind speed at 2 m, saturation and actual vapour pressure, slope of vapour pressure curve, psychrometric constant, etc.
